REALCal Bookings Consent Document

SMS and Text Messaging Opt-In

By providing your phone number and completing your profile, you expressly consent to receive automated messaging (SMS or text messaging) from REALCal Bookings. These messages are primarily used to provide you with:

  • Booking confirmations and status updates.
  • Physical smart lock access codes for your stay.
  • Urgent property-related notifications (e.g., maintenance or safety alerts).

Terms and Conditions

  • Frequency: Message frequency varies based on your booking activity.
  • Costs: Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.
  • Opt-Out: You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Just text "STOP" to our short code or contact our support team. After you send the SMS message "STOP" to us, we will send you an SMS message to confirm that you have been unsubscribed.
  • Privacy: We respect your privacy. Mobile information will not be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes.

Verification of Consent

This document serves as clinical evidence of your explicit invitation and consent to receive these communications, recorded in your user profile under "Toll-free-accept".
